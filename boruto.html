Boruto Uzumaki

Profile

Full Name Boruto Uzumaki
Age 12–16
Clan Uzumaki Clan
Rank Genin
Team Team Konohamaru

Biography

Boruto Uzumaki is the son of Naruto Uzumaki, the Seventh Hokage, and Hinata Hyuga. Although he respects his father, Boruto initially resents him for prioritizing the village over their family. Over time, Boruto matures, learns the true meaning of being a shinobi, and strives to make his own path beyond his father's legacy.

Abilities

  • Shadow Clone Technique
  • Rasengan (including Vanishing Rasengan)
  • Jōgan (Unique Dōjutsu)
  • Nature Transformation: Lightning & Wind Release
