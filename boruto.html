Boruto Uzumaki

Profile

Full Name: Boruto Uzumaki
Age: 12–16
Clan: Uzumaki Clan
Rank: Genin
Team: Team Konohamaru

Biography

Boruto is the son of Naruto Uzumaki, the Seventh Hokage, and Hinata Hyuga. Initially struggling with his father’s responsibilities, he matures into a capable ninja and learns to forge his own path, embracing teamwork, loyalty, and courage.

Abilities

Shadow Clone Technique

Creates multiple copies to overwhelm enemies or for training.

Rasengan

Spinning chakra sphere; includes Vanishing Rasengan for advanced combat.

Jōgan

A unique dōjutsu that detects chakra and dimensional portals.

Nature Transformations

Lightning & Wind Release, enhancing offensive capabilities.
